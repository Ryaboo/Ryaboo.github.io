Musical Instruments!

With all the musical instruments in the world, its hard to choose a specific one to dedicate your free time to. With this table below, you'll be able to clearly see the different types of musical instruments there can be in the world of music!

Electric Guitar
Drums
Trumpet
Saxophone
Piano
Xylophone
Electric Bass
Clarinet
Flute
Harp

All of these instruments are just some of the ones people tend to pick up. Obviously drums, piano, guitar, and bass are the most common for people looking to start a band or pick up an easy instrument. Although, there are plenty more instruments than the ones listed that many people use in many songs. For example, a synthesizer is a common instrument used in 80's music that people often overlook. Much like a piano, it can be played on a keyboard and is great for making unique, retro sounds!

If you are looking to create music, it can be sometimes hard to create music on your own. Having a second or more opinions can form and shape a sound to near perfection.
