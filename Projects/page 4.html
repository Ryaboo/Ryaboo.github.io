Starting a Band!

Starting a band can be a difficult thing to do. Whether it be people not interested in music, lack of equipment, or even lack of motivation or social interaction, these all are troubling factors that play into making a band. Given the right circumstances and a drive to create music...

What Do you Need?

Equipment is a very important factor that relies on other people or your own self to prepare. A good place to start is to look at pawnshops or online stores that sell older, used equipment. Although this isn't always reliable and new, this gives a cheaper option to buying new gear that can cost thousands of dollars! The list below describes some of the more important items to buy!

  • Amplications for Sound
  • Recording Devices
  • Pedals or Programs for altering sound
  • Instruments themselves
  • A positive attitude!

Having all of these ensure a successful attempt at making a band! With this you are now ready to start a band!
